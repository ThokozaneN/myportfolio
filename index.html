How I Became a Digital Storyteller

Every great adventure has a beginning

2022

It started with curiosity - taking apart my MySpace profile, tweaking HTML tags without understanding what they did. The magic of changing colors and layouts with code fascinated me.

2023

My first freelance gig - a local bakery needed a website. I stayed up for 72 hours straight, fueled by coffee and determination. The site was basic, but the owner's smile when she saw it? Priceless.

2024

The turning point. A complex project forced me to learn JavaScript properly. The struggle was real, but when the interactive map finally worked, I realized coding wasn't just a skill - it was a superpower.

Present

Now I craft digital experiences that blend form and function. Each project is a new story to tell, a new problem to solve. The journey continues, one line of code at a time.
