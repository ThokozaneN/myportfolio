How I Became a Digital Storyteller

Every great adventure has a beginning

2022

It began with me messing around in code, breaking stuff just to see what would happen. I didn’t know half the tags I was changing, but damn, watching colors and layouts shift because of my hacks hooked me hard. That’s where it all started—pure, messy obsession..

2023

Stayed up 72 hours straight, fueled by coffee and stubbornness, building a demo website just to sharpen my skills. The site was simple, but it was proof I could deliver. That grind? Priceless..

2024

The turning point. A complex project forced me to learn JavaScript properly. The struggle was real, but when the interactive map finally worked, I realized coding wasn't just a skill - it was a superpower.

Present

Now I craft digital experiences that blend form and function. Each project is a new story to tell, a new problem to solve. The journey continues, one line of code at a time.

The Person Behind the Code

More than just a developer

I didn’t grow up with tech handed to me, I grew up with curiosity, struggle, and Wi-Fi. While most teens were chasing trends, I was chasing skills. I taught myself how to build websites from the ground up, often staying up till sunrise learning through trial, error, and raw obsession. No Computer Science degree, just determination and a secondhand device that barely kept up.

My creativity couldn’t be boxed in, though. That’s why I launched Close Division, my creative agency where web development, graphic design, and digital marketing meet. Every brand I work with, I treat like it’s my own: making sure their online presence doesn’t just look good, but feels right. Whether I’m coding clean interfaces or directing a visual story, I’m about crafting digital experiences that connect.

What drives me isn’t hype — it’s real progress. Solving problems for real people. Helping small businesses show up like big brands. Turning abstract ideas into something functional, dope, and impactful. I don’t just want to succeed, I want to build, uplift, and own every step of the journey.
